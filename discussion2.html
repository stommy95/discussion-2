S Town

Structure of Podcast

A TV show format of like a crime mystery. This podcast is also more of a reporter/ journalistic format where most of it is the main reporter talking to john about his s-town and a murder.

Who is doing most of the talking?

It is a split with john rambling awhile however the reporter has a lot of questions and explanantions throughout

For the most part it seems to be a live conversation however the reporter has some scripted questions and a scripted intro.

Interviews?

The whole podcast is a glorified interview with John and the reporter.

Serial Podcast

Structure of Podcast

This podcast is much of the same with an investigative journalism murder mystery. Sarah the main investigator gives background information and what she has done to set up for this investigation.

Who is doing most of the talking?

Mostly the main reporter sarah is talking but she has interviews with people to either further her hypothesis and to give the audience evidence and background.

Is it scripted?

Sarah's part of the reporter is clearly scripted but the interviews is clearly not scripted.

Interviews?

Many interviews starting with a few interviews with her son and friends about recoalition. Then many interviews with victims/ witnesses.

Sound effects and music?

Only some music which is only sound effect, besides interviews which is sound that is imported.
